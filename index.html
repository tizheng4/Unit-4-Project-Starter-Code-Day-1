Early Life

  • Born in Würzburg, Germany
  • His mom played professional basketball and his dad played handball
  • Originally played handball and tennis
  • Started playing basketball at around 13
  • Declared for the NBA draft in 1998 and was selected 9th overall that year

Notable Accomplishments

  • 2011 NBA Champion
  • NBA Finals MVP: 2011
  • NBA Most Valuable Player: 2007
  • 14x NBA All-Star: 2002–2012, 2014–2015, 2019
  • 2x All-NBA Team: 2001–2012
